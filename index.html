SOLAR SYSTEM

The Sun

The star at the center of our system.

Advantage: Energy source for life.
Disadvantage: Radiation and heat.

01. Mercury

77 Million km from Earth.

Advantage: Rich in minerals.
Disadvantage: No atmosphere, wild heat.

02. Venus

41 Million km from Earth.

Advantage: Earth-like gravity.
Disadvantage: Toxic air and acid rain.

03. Earth

Our Home Planet.

Advantage: Perfect for life.
Disadvantage: Natural disasters.

04. Mars

225 Million km from Earth.

Advantage: Potential for colony.
Disadvantage: Dust storms & radiation.

05. Jupiter

628 Million km from Earth.

Advantage: Gravity protects Earth.
Disadvantage: No solid ground.

06. Saturn

1.2 Billion km from Earth.

Advantage: Beautiful rings/moons.
Disadvantage: High pressure.

07. Uranus

2.6 Billion km from Earth.

Advantage: Cold science research.
Disadvantage: Freezing winds.

08. Neptune

4.3 Billion km from Earth.

Advantage: Methane riches.
Disadvantage: Farthest and darkest.
